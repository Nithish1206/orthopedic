WHY NOW ?

Fear of the unknown can lead patients to delay treatment, surgery and diagnosis. While surgery can be scary the impact of waiting can have severe consequences on your physical health, mental health, family, finances and quality of life. Delay can cause anxiety, depression and other emotional distress. Physically delay can worsen your condition resulting in more complicated surgery, longer therapy and a decline in overall health. Putting off diagnosis can lead to trips to the ER, frequent visits to doctors, development of new issues and other unintended consequences. This health spiral takes a toll financially, physically and mentally on you and your family.

DON'T DELAY YOUR TREATMENT

Risks of delayed Treatment
  • Minor Problems become Major Problems
  • Recovery Time Increases
  • Quality of Life Declines
  • Compensatory/New Problems Develop
Benefits of treatment
  • Return to Physical Activity
  • Rejoin Family Activities
  • Reduction in Pain
  • Quality of Life Improves

For Individuals with an ACL tear the chance of a meniscal tear increases by 2% with each week of delay !.
